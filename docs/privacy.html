Araxis — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

Araxis ("the app") is a business tool used by service/installation companies to manage their staff and operations. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Information we collect

  • Phone number — used to sign you in (one-time SMS code) via Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile — your name, role, and team, as set up by your business's administrator.
  • Operational data — service calls, hardware inventory, crew assignments, and payout amounts that you and your colleagues enter to run the business.

How we use it

Data is used solely to provide the app's functionality for your business. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing.

Where it is stored

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) on Google's infrastructure, protected by access rules that limit data to your business's authorized staff.

Data retention & deletion

Data is retained while your account is active. To request access to, or deletion of, your data, contact your business administrator or email sleeman.nabwani@gmail.com.
